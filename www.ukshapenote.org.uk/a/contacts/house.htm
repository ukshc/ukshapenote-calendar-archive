Monthly Local and House Singings

The best way of learning about this type of music is to experience it first hand. This can be done either at regular singing days organised around the country, or by going to local singing groups, usually held monthly in churches, halls and  people's houses. Usually informal, these are a great way to learn or 'workshop' new pieces, and are open to anyone who would like to attend. Some have their own websites.

NB:  The MSN maps currently available do not give a great deal of detail, thus the Google or the Multimap references are preferable.
