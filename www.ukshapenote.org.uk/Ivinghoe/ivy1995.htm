Shapenote Singing Day
 
Ivinghoe Town Hall
Ivinghoe
Buckingamshire
Sunday, 10th September 1995
 

NH(2) : Northern Harmony, 2nd ed. 1990
NH : Northern Harmony 3rd ed. 1995

Bob Lilley welcomed the singers and Sheila Girling Smith led NH(2) 30. Leaders: Bob Lilley NH(2) 62; Steve Fletcher NH(2) 87; Bernard Collard 86; Tony Singleton 155; singer unknown NH(2) 76; Bob Lilley NH 150; Tony Singleton 183; Steve Fletcher NH 86; Sheila Girling Smith 31t; Simon Chadwick 454; Tony Singleton 300; Bob Lilley NH 124 and NH 66. The session closed with the singing of 299.

 

Compiled from records kept by Bernard Collard.
