East Midlands Sacred Harp Convention
Kegworth Village Hall
Leicestershire, UK
24 and 25 April 2010
 

Saturday 24 April

The East Midlands Sacred Harp Convention was called to order by Ian West leading song on page 479. Gillian White offered the opening prayer.

The following officers were elected or appointed to serve: Chairman - Ian West; Vice Chairman - Helen Brown; Secretaries - Margaret Gillanders and Rosalind Oldham.

Leaders: Helen Brown 34b; Dave Richardson 31b; Margaret Gillanders 171; Ruth Steggles 49b; Ted Brown 47t; Sarah West 288; Hannah Land 56t; Chris Brown 38t; Sharon Langridge 276; Rosalind Oldham 84; Michael Walker (for  Jacqui Selby-Macleod) 131b; Judy Whiting 354t; Maria Wallace 142; Peter
Knudssen 148; Steve Fletcher 131t; Ian West 54t (CB); Gillian White 128  (CB); Helen Brown 82t; Ruth Steggles 300; Ted Brown 303; Margaret Gillanders 559 (CB); Sarah West 432.

Dave Richardson called the class back leading 111t. Leaders: Hannah Land 557 (CB); Chris Brown 30t (CB); Sharon Langridge 35; Rosalind Oldham 312b; Michael Walker 170; Judy Whiting 318; Maria Wallace 448t; Peter Knudssen 350;  Steve Fletcher 117; Gillian White 107; Cath Tyler 296; Mick Verrier 106 (CB; Claire Singleton 270;  Phil Tyler 139; Ian West 500;  Helen Brown 140 (CB); Ruth Steggles 505 (CB; Margaret Gillanders 225t; Hannah Land 452; Chris Brown 466; Rosalind Oldham 571 (CB); Sharon Langridge 159; Michael Walker 377; Judy Whiting 460; Maria Wallace 183; Ruth Steggles 198.  Martin Williams asked the blessing for the mid-day meal.

Sarah West brought the class together with song on page 89. Leaders: Dave Richardson 488t (CB); Cath Tyler 497 (CB); Gillian White 573 (CB);Claire Singleton 124; Phil Tyler 477; Helen Brown 277; Ian West 68b; Hannah Land 475; Ruth Steggles 488b (CB); Mick Verrier 344; Maria Wallace 481;Michael Walker 56b; Margaret Gillanders 542; Steve Fletcher 65. Ian West and Helen Brown led 330b as the closing song.  Gillian White offered the closing prayer and the class was dismissed.

Sunday 25 April

The Sunday session of the East Midlands Sacred Harp Convention was called to order by Ian West leading 52t. Ken Baddley offered the opening prayer. Leaders: Helen Brown 492; Dave Richardson 48t; Ruth Steggles 36b; Ted Brown 503; Margaret Gillanders 203; Claire Singleton 168; Michael Walker 172; Judy Whiting 77b; Hannah Land 434;  Nick Hall 147t; Rosalind Oldham 68b; Sarah West 498;  Chris Brown 38b;  Rachel Jordan 300; Ken Baddley 121; Cath Tyler 47t; Richard Percival 210; Phil Tyler 98 (CB); Ian West 575 (CB); Helen Brown 392.

RECESS

Dave Richardson called the class back to order leading 345b; MargaretGillanders 99;Ted Brown 496; Ruth Steggles 573 (CB); Claire Singleton 162; Nick Hall 455; Judy Whiting 222; Rosalind Oldham 127; Sarah West 224; Chris Brown 567; Rachel Jordan 501; Ken Baddley 488b (CB);  Cath Tyler 560 (CB); Sheila Girling Macadam 330b; Richard Percival 479; Edwin Macadam 441; Michael Walker 435 (CB). 

The Memorial Lesson was conducted by Hannah Land and song on page 448b was led in honour of the following deceased: Jim Clay, Johnny Collins, Audrey Nathan, Gordon Jones, Sally Smyth, Pete Chopping, Marie Holloway, Meriel and Eddie Byrne, Alan Pringle, Wendy Hately, David Clarence, Alf Reeves-Easton, Glynne Williams, David Lee, Eric Mogensen, Eugene Cerveny (OH), Geoff Hayto,Victoria Bolles (MA), Jancis Wilson, Priscilla Quayle.

Dave Richardson reconvened the class leading 87. The singing resumed with Ted Brown leading 111b. Chairman--Ian West:
