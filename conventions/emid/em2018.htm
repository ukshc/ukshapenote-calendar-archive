Sarah spoke for the sick and housebound and led 330b for the following: Ottis Sides, Velton Chafin, David Elliott, Reginald Brett, Darele Angwin, Trish Stadward, Curtis Owen, Rev Michael Pain, Rosie and Andy, Derek Gillott, Stuart Russell, Sister Pippa, Roy Hollowell, Andy Spence, Paula Mogensen, and Idy Kiser.
